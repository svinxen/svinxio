Services

I design and build responsive, accessible, and performant web interfaces using modern frameworks and tools.

Creating intuitive user experiences with a focus on user-centered design principles and aesthetics.

Developing scalable and maintainable web applications tailored to client needs and business goals.

Providing bespoke solutions, including building custom furniture and enclosures, driven by a passion for creation and improvement.

A Path Shaped by Questions

I've always been drawn to understanding how things work. For 15 years, I worked as a service manager, a role I enjoyed because it allowed me to support people in their everyday lives. When I began exploring new career paths, I realized I could combine what I love with my profession: technology and development. Today, I build websites, apps, and systems, but also furniture, enclosures, and practical solutions for my animals. It all comes from the same drive - to create, improve, and understand.

Beyond the Code

During the Swedish summer months, I spend as much time as I can outdoors. Carrying my sleep system on my back, I search for the perfect spot to call home for the night. The wind, the birds, the rhythm of water, it all slows me down. Time with my dogs, time with the ground beneath me feet. Nature is where I truly return to myself. It grounds me, recharges me, and reminds me who I am beyond all the noise. It shapes how I see the world, the foundation for how I think, feel, and create.

who?

Jag har alltid gillat att förstå hur saker fungerar och att hitta lösningar på problem.

I 15 år arbetade jag som service manager – ett jobb jag trivdes med just för att jag fick hjälpa människor i deras vardag.

När jag tittade vidare på andra karriärmöjligheter insåg jag att jag kunde kombinera det jag gillar med mitt yrke: datorer och utveckling.

Idag bygger jag hemsidor, appar, system. Men jag bygger också möbler, burar och andra saker till mina djur. Det är samma driv – att skapa, förbättra och förstå.
