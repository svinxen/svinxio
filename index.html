Precision meets purpose.

Throughout my life, I’ve faced challenges that pushed me to prove myself and persevere. One memorable experience was when I decided last summer (2024) to climb the highest mountain in Sweden with my small 5kg dog, despite everyone telling me it would be too difficult for both of us. I trained for hard, and we reached the summit before others who doubted us. This taught me the value of hard work, resilience, and overcoming obstacles— principles that I carry with me into every challenge.
